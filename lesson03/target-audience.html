Target Audience

A target audience are people of different age groups with various interests. Here are two personas with different interests that both enjoy using Wacky Weather:

Mariah:

"Hi I am Mariah! I am 28 years old, and from Texas. I am a traveling photographer, and use wacky weather all the time. Wacky Weather is super helpful when I am planning my vacations, this weather app helps me know what kind of weather I should plan for, and what kind of clothes I should bring with me."

Porter:

"Hi I'm Porter. I am from Oregon, and am almost 40. Weather patterns have always fascinated me. I check the weather multiple times a day. Wacky Weather is one of my favorite ways to check weather patterns, and is usually fairly accurate. I also love predicting future weather patterns, and then comparing my predictions with Wacky Weathers predictions. This is a great resource.

Here are several different scenarios that would help us understand the goal of the website better:

1) What is the weather/temperature for today?
2) How strong are the wind levels today?
3) What kind of weather should I expect tomorrow?
4) What kind of clothing should I wear this week? Is it warm enough for sandals?
5) Should I expect rain later today?
